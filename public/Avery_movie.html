The Best Books of All Time

The Topmost Books According to Avery

Nightmares Series

A funny yet slightly creepy series about a boy and his new family who face creatures who are (literally) the things of nightmares.

Percy Jackson Series

A hilarious series about a kid who is the child of the Greek god Poseidon who faces Greek monsters with a grin and some friends to save the world.

Dragon's Egg (Del Rey Impact)

A Sci-Fi book about intelligent life on a nuetron star who are living 1000 times faster than humans. They develop technology that even humans hadn't created yet, all within a human lifetime.

Serafina Series

A heartfelt series about a young girl who struggles to find out who she really is. All while fighting evil creatures who want to kill her and her mother, who are magical creatures who can turn into big cats.
